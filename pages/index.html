Features

Everything you need to develop photos

📁

Library

Import folders, browse photos with thumbnails, search your entire catalog, and view EXIF metadata.

◐

Develop

Non-destructive editing with Basic, Tone Curve, HSL, Calibration, Crop, Transform, Lens, Detail, and Effects.

◎

RAW support

Edit CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG, and more. Full raw pipeline powered by rawloader.

↺

History & presets

Save and recall edit states with presets, checkpoints, and a full edit history in a SQLite catalog.

✦

Spot heal

Remove distractions with spot healing tools integrated into the develop workflow.

↗

Export

Batch export to JPEG, PNG, or TIFF with quality, resize, and upscale options.

Your photos stay yours

Aramis Editor runs entirely on your machine. No cloud uploads, no telemetry, no accounts required.

  • Local catalog & thumbnails
  • XMP sidecar support
  • Open source (MIT)
